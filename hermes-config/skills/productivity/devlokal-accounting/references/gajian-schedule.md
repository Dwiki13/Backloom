# Gajian Schedule Reference

## Rules

- **Gajian date: 28th of every month**
- **If 28th falls on Saturday/Sunday: gajian moves to the preceding Friday**
- **Recording starts the day AFTER gajian**
- **NEVER calculate independently — always confirm with KII**
