## Match Type Strategy

- **Phrase match** — Best balance of reach and relevance for Indonesian market. Higher CTR, lower CPC.
- **Broad match** — Avoid for budget-constrained campaigns. Bleeds budget on irrelevant queries.
- **Exact match** — Use for proven high-converting keywords only.

## Budget Guidelines (IDR)

- Testing phase: Rp50,000–100,000/day
- Scale phase: increase 20-30% weekly if CPA is acceptable
- Always start with Phrase match + negative keywords before increasing budget
