# Follow-Up Message Templates — Indonesian Service Businesses (Prototype-First)

## When to Use
- User reports leads ghosting after initial WA contact
- User has warm leads who said "still considering" or "pikirkan dulu"
- User has leads who asked for pricing but didn't respond after quote
- User wants to start outreach to new cold leads

## Approach: Prototype-First (Updated June 21, 2026)

**Old approach:** Long intro chat → FU1 send portfolio → FU2
**New approach:** Send prototype link directly + 1 sentence personal → FU1 → FU2

**Why:** People don't read long WA messages. They scan 2-3 lines. A prototype link is worth 100 words of description. Faster to send (1-2 min vs 3-5 min per contact), and filters better (only people who click and respond are qualified).

## Comparison: Old vs New Approach

- Message length: 6-8 lines → 2-3 lines
- Proof: None → Direct prototype link
- CTA: "Gratis konsultasi" → "Kalau cocok, bisa saya buatkan"
- Time per contact: 3-5 min → 1-2 min
- Response rate: ~10% → Target 25-35%

## General Rules

1. Don't offer discounts in first message
2. Personalize 1 sentence — mention their business name
3. Keep it short — 2-3 lines max for intro
4. Give an easy out — "kabari kalau sudah siap"
5. Max 2 follow-ups — after that, mark as lost
6. Timing: Intro → FU1 (day 3) → FU2 (day 7, final)
7. Track leads in spreadsheet or WA Business labels
